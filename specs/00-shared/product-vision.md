# 1. Vision Statement

Requira aims to become the intelligent quality gate for software requirements by helping organizations evaluate whether their requirement artifacts are complete, consistent, and ready for development before engineering work begins.

The platform is built on the belief that better requirements lead to better software. Instead of replacing Business Analysts or Product Managers, Requira enhances their review process by identifying overlooked gaps, risks, and ambiguities, enabling teams to make more informed decisions with greater confidence.

---

# 2. Mission Statement

Our mission is to improve software delivery by providing an AI-assisted requirement assessment platform that helps teams discover missing information, reduce requirement-related rework, and improve development readiness through explainable recommendations backed by requirement engineering best practices.

---

# 3. The Problem We Are Solving

Software projects rarely fail because requirements are not written.

They fail because requirements are often incomplete.

Organizations already invest significant effort in producing:

* Business Requirement Documents (BRDs)
* Product Requirement Documents (PRDs)
* Software Requirement Specifications (SRS)
* User Stories
* Meeting Notes
* Functional Specifications

However, very few teams have a structured process to answer an important question:

> **"Are these requirements actually ready for development?"**

Important information is frequently missed during requirement analysis, including:

* Business rules
* User permissions
* Validation logic
* Notifications
* Error handling
* Edge cases
* Non-functional requirements
* External integrations

These issues are usually discovered later during development or testing, resulting in avoidable rework, delivery delays, and increased project costs.

Requira exists to reduce these risks before implementation begins.

---

# 4. Product Vision

Requira should become the first tool teams use after completing requirement gathering and before assigning work to engineering.

The platform should serve as an intelligent review layer that strengthens existing requirement artifacts rather than replacing current documentation practices.

Instead of asking teams to change how they write requirements, Requira adapts to existing workflows by auditing whatever requirement artifacts already exist.

---

# 5. Product Positioning

Requira is positioned as an AI-assisted Requirements Readiness Assessment Platform.

It is not:

* A project management system.
* A documentation platform.
* A requirement generation tool.
* A chatbot.
* A replacement for Business Analysts.

It is a decision-support platform that helps software teams evaluate requirement quality before development begins.

---

# 6. Product Philosophy

The product is built around five core principles.

## Review Before Build

Every software project should undergo a structured requirement review before implementation begins.

---

## AI Supports Human Expertise

Artificial intelligence should enhance the work of Business Analysts and Product Managers rather than replacing them.

Every recommendation generated by Requira is advisory and should be reviewed by the project team.

---

## Explain Every Recommendation

Every finding should answer:

* What was identified?
* Why is it important?
* What evidence supports it?
* What action is recommended?

Recommendations without explanations should never be presented to users.

---

## Focus on Business Value

Every feature added to Requira should help answer one business question:

> **"Are these requirements ready for development?"**

Features that do not contribute to this objective should not be included in the MVP.

---

## Build Practical Software

Requira is designed to solve a real engineering problem using practical technology and realistic implementation scope.

The product prioritizes usefulness over technical complexity.

---

# 7. Core Product Workflow

The intended user journey is straightforward.

1. User creates a project.
2. User uploads requirement documents.
3. Requira processes the documents.
4. Requirements are extracted and analyzed.
5. Potential gaps and risks are identified.
6. Clarification questions and test scenarios are generated.
7. Requirement Health is calculated.
8. A Development Readiness Recommendation is produced.
9. The user reviews findings and updates the requirements if necessary.
10. A professional audit report can be exported.

The workflow is iterative, allowing users to continuously improve requirement quality through repeated audits.

---

# 8. Unique Value Proposition

Most existing AI tools focus on creating requirement documents.

Requira focuses on evaluating them.

Rather than asking:

> "How can we generate more requirements?"

Requira asks:

> "Have we captured enough information to begin development with confidence?"

This shift from generation to assessment defines the product's identity.

---

# 9. Product Differentiators

Requira differentiates itself through several core capabilities.

### AI-Assisted Requirement Assessment

The platform evaluates existing requirement artifacts instead of generating them.

### Explainable Findings

Every gap, risk, and recommendation includes supporting evidence and reasoning.

### Requirement Health

Users receive an overall view of the quality and completeness of their requirements.

### Development Readiness Recommendation

Instead of presenting raw analysis, Requira summarizes whether the project appears ready for development while making it clear that the recommendation is advisory.

### Human-Centered Decision Support

The platform assists software teams in making informed decisions without replacing human judgment.

# 10. Long-Term Product Vision

The MVP focuses on general software requirement assessment.

Future versions may expand to include:

* Industry-specific audit frameworks
* Requirement traceability
* Version comparison
* Collaboration workflows
* Jira and Azure DevOps integrations
* Compliance auditing
* Continuous requirement monitoring
* Team analytics
* AI-assisted requirement refinement

These enhancements should build upon the same core philosophy established by the MVP.

---

# 11. Product Success

Requira succeeds when users consistently describe it as a tool that helps them identify issues they would otherwise have missed before development started.

The product should become part of the requirement review process rather than replacing it.

Success is measured not by the amount of AI-generated content, but by the number of requirement issues identified early enough to prevent downstream rework.

---

# 12. Product Identity

Requira is an engineering productivity platform designed to improve software delivery through better requirement validation.

Its purpose is to provide software teams with the confidence to begin development based on structured, explainable assessments of requirement quality.

The product is intentionally focused on one responsibility:

**Helping teams make better-informed decisions about whether their current requirements are ready for development.**
